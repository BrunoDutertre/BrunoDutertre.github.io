Formal Requirements Analysis of an Avionics Control System

Bruno Dutertre and Victoria Stavridou,
IEEE Transactions on Software Engineering ,
Vol. 25, No. 5, pp. 267-278, May 1997.

Abstract

This paper reports on a formal analysis experiment involving an avionics control system. We describe a method for specifying and verifying real-time systems with PVS . The experiment involves the formalization of the functional and safety requirements as well as its multilevel verification. First level verification demonstrates the consistency of thespecifications whilst the second level shows that certain safety properties are satisfied by the specifications. We critically analyze methodological issues of large scale verification and propose some practical ways of structuring verification activities for optimizing the benefits.

The work described in this paper was a major part of the SafeFM project.
